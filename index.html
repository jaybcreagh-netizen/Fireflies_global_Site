ONE MISSION. A GLOBAL RIDE.

It started over two decades ago with a single, grueling bike ride across the Alps. Today, we are a global community of riders, creatives, and friends, united by one motto.

In 2001, with the backing of RSA Films, the first Fireflies Tour was born. That spirit caught fire, inspiring new tours across the world from California's coast with Fireflies West to the raw wilderness of Patagonia. Each ride supports vital cancer charities in its region, continuing the mission we started all those years ago.

OUR IMPACT

Every kilometer pedaled, every mountain climbed, is in service of a greater cause. Together, we have raised a significant total for life-saving cancer research and treatment across the globe.
